# Day 21 – Digital Privacy Dashboard

## Objective

Generate an interactive Digital Privacy Dashboard using Claude AI.

## Features

- Digital Footprint Score
- Privacy Score
- Exposure Heatmap
- Company Exposure Ranking
- Data Collection Matrix
- Risk Radar
- Digital Twin Profile
- Most Valuable Data Assets
- Privacy Improvement Simulator
- Final Verdict

## Privacy Findings

- Reviewed digital footprint.
- Analyzed company exposure.
- Studied estimated tracking surface.
- Explored risk radar.
- Identified valuable personal data assets.

## Learnings

- Digital services contribute to an overall online footprint.
- Parent companies can aggregate data across multiple services.
- Separating Facts from Estimates is important in privacy analysis.
- Privacy dashboards help visualize exposure and potential improvements.
